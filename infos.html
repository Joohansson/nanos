About

What is this?

Buying a separate domain name for every project can be expensive, usually around $10-50/year/domain.We give you the option to use our domain names 1na.no or nanos.cc, with each subdomain leading to the IP or hostname of your choice. This can for example be a PC with dynamic DNS, a cloud server / Nano node with static IP or free web hosting at Github Pages.Find out more about Nano at nano.org

How does it work?

1. You make a request and a manual review will be made to ensure you meet our criteria.
2. We let Cloudflare handle the DNS mapping. It includes improved speed, their SSL certificate and our subdomain for 1 year.
3. You may need changes at your end for example setting up github pages or link the new domain in your web server.
4. You will be notified before expiration with the option to renew your subdomain.

music.nanos.cc redirects to joohansson.github.io with the use of a CNAME file.
Other valid examples are 1.1.1.1, xyz.dyndns.org, mynode.io, myproject.com

Does it cost anything?

There is no obligation to pay for your subdomain. However, running this service is NOT free and effortless.
We suggest an annual fair Nano donation of 1-3 USD equivalent to help us keep this alive and as a gesture of appreciation.

Benefits

1. You save cost, the hassle of domain setup, and no fighting with SSL settings in your web server.
2. You get a cool and easy to remember domain name tailored for Nano
3. Speed optimizations, DDoS protection, rate limiting and site cache powered by Cloudflare
4. Your real hostname or IP will be hidden beneath the new domain and cloudflare
5. (Optional) Listing on this page as a free advertising of your project and increased reach in search engines.
